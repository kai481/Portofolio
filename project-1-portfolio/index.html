Our Story

Born from ember, refined by craft.

Ember & Oak started with a simple belief: the best meals come from the simplest methods. Our founders — a former Michelin-trained chef and a local farmer — combined their passions to create a dining experience that honors the ingredient above all.

Every dish on our menu is designed around what's in season. We work with eight partner farms within 50km of Bucharest to source produce at its peak. The result is food that's honest, vibrant, and deeply satisfying.
