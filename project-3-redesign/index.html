RoleLead UI Designer
Timeline8 Weeks
ToolsFigma, Principle
01 — Overview

The brief.

The Client

FinFlow is a mid-market banking app serving 350K+ users. Despite solid functionality, their app felt dated and had an industry-trailing NPS score of 18.

The Goal

Redesign the core experience — dashboard, transactions, transfers, and cards — to reduce cognitive load, increase engagement, and modernize the visual language.

The Outcome

After launch, FinFlow saw a 42% increase in daily active users, NPS jumped to 61, and support tickets dropped by 35%.

02 — Problems Identified

What wasn't working.

01

Visual Clutter

The dashboard crammed 14 different data points above the fold with no visual hierarchy. Users couldn't find their balance quickly.

Before
After
02

Confusing Navigation

5-level deep hamburger menu with inconsistent back-button behavior. Users averaged 6+ taps to complete a simple transfer.

Taps to Transfer
6 taps
2 taps
03

Outdated Visual Design

Flat grey UI with system fonts and no personality. The app felt generic compared to modern fintech competitors like Revolut and N26.

04

Poor Feedback

No loading states, no success confirmations, no micro-interactions. Users were left guessing whether actions completed.
