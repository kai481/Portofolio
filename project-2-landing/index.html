Our Story

Born from a love of the perfect cup.

Verdant Roasters started in a garage in Portland with a single Probat roaster and an obsession: what does the perfect cup taste like? Seven years later, we've traveled to 14 countries, built direct relationships with small-hold farmers, and served over 2 million cups.

Great coffee is more than a commodity — it's a craft, a connection, and a daily act of care.
