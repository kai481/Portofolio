02 — About Me

Passionate about building things that live on the web.

I'm Alex, a junior web developer and designer based in Romania with a keen eye for detail and a love for clean, modern aesthetics. I started my journey in web development because I was fascinated by the idea of turning a blank screen into something interactive and alive.

I focus on writing semantic HTML, crafting pixel-perfect CSS, and building interactive experiences with JavaScript. Every project is an opportunity to learn something new and push my skills further. I believe great design isn't just about looks — it's about how it works and how it makes people feel.
